## Frequently asked

**Is there a contract?**
12-month minimum, then month-to-month. Cancel anytime after your first year, no fees.

**What if I move?**
Halstead moves with you. We'll uninstall and reinstall at your new address (within our service area) for a flat fee.

**Do I own the equipment?**
Yes. You bought it. It's yours. Take it with you when you move or keep it if you cancel.

**What happens if I cancel?**
After year one, your monitoring stops at the end of your billing month. Equipment continues to work as standalone cameras with local recording on the hub.

**What if my internet goes down?**
Pro Monitoring tier includes a cellular backup hub — your system stays connected even if internet is out. Other tiers continue recording locally.

**Are recordings encrypted?**
Yes. End-to-end encryption from camera to cloud, with industry-standard protections at rest.
