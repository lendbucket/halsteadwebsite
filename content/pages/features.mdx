# Smart features. Built for the way you actually live.

Most security apps blow up your phone with motion alerts, flag every passing car, and scare you out of using them. Halstead is engineered differently.

---

## Smart filtering that actually works

### Two-camera verification
Most security alarms trigger on a single sensor. Halstead requires corroboration from a second camera or sensor before escalating to an alarm. This single feature eliminates 70% of false alarms.

### AI motion classification (Premium tier)
Person, vehicle, package, animal, or other. Subscribe only to the categories that matter to you. Person at 3am? Notify. Squirrel at 3am? Skip.

### Storm mode
Severe weather alert from the National Weather Service automatically calms motion sensitivity. No more 50 false alerts during a thunderstorm.

### Smart family check-ins
Your kids come home from school. Halstead recognizes them, sends you a "Sarah is home, 3:47pm" notification — without triggering a scary alarm.

---

## Living-with-it features

### Vacation mode
One toggle. Increases sensitivity. Sets longer retention. Sends you a daily digest. Alerts your trusted neighbor list. Returns to normal when you tap it off.

### Visitor pass
Your cleaner comes Tuesday at 10am. Generate a visitor pass that records video for your archive but doesn't blast you with alerts during their window. Same for the dog walker, the contractor, the in-laws.

### Doorbell scripts
Set pre-recorded responses for the doorbell. "We're not interested, please leave." "Please leave packages on the porch." Auto-respond when nobody's home.

### Historical timeline
"What happened at my house yesterday from 3-5pm?" Halstead AI summarizes everything that occurred in any window — visits, motion, deliveries — in plain language.

### Halstead Watch (opt-in)
Share blurred motion events with neighbors who are also Halstead customers. A community watch network without the toxicity of Citizen or Nextdoor — just neighbors helping neighbors stay aware.

## When something does happen

### Smart escalation
Motion detected. Push notification sent. No response in 90 seconds? Halstead calls you. No answer? Calls your emergency contact. (Pro Monitoring tier) No answer there? Dispatches authorities.

### Two-way audio
Talk to whoever's at your door from anywhere. "I'll be right down" or "Please leave the package on the bench."

### Package alerts
Halstead AI distinguishes a package being placed (delivery confirmation) from a package being removed (theft alert). Different notifications for different actions.

### Cellular backup (Pro tier)
Internet down? Power glitched? Halstead Pro Monitoring includes cellular backup that keeps your system online when nothing else is.

## For the household, not just the homeowner

### Multi-user access
Family members each get their own login. Permissions per user — kids can see live, parents can change settings.

### Live multi-camera grid (Premium)
See all your cameras at once. Pinch to zoom. Tap any camera to focus.

### Audio recording opt-in
Audio is off by default. You explicitly enable audio recording per camera, with state-specific consent prompts to keep you compliant with recording laws.

Halstead generates the certificate of monitoring and auto-files with your home insurance carrier annually. Most carriers offer 5-15% discounts on monitored homes — Halstead does the paperwork so you actually get the discount.

0% interest, 12 months. No need to put $400-800 of cameras on a credit card.

### Real estate sale report
Selling your home? Halstead generates a professional report on your installed equipment for your listing. Buyers love seeing professional security already in place.
