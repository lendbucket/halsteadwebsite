# Halstead Marketing Site — Roadmap

The marketing site evolves alongside the platform. As features ship in the platform, marketing pages get added or updated to sell those features.

## Phase 1 — V1 launch site (Days 18-21, ~16 hours)

Goal: A public site that legitimizes Halstead Security and converts the first batch of homeowners and dealer applicants.

### Pages
- [P1] Home page (hero, value prop, social proof placeholder, dual CTA)
- [P1] Plans & Pricing (Basic, Premium tiers)
- [P1] Equipment (camera kit overview)
- [P1] Become a Dealer (dealer pitch, application CTA)
- [P1] About (founder story, mission)
- [P1] Contact (form, support routing)
- [P1] Privacy Policy
- [P1] Terms of Service
- [P1] FAQ

### Marketing infrastructure
- [P1] Domain DNS pointed at Vercel
- [P1] Contact form → Resend / SendGrid → email
- [P1] Dealer application form → routes to platform application
- [P1] Plausible or PostHog analytics installed
- [P1] OG meta tags, sitemap, robots.txt
- [P1] Mobile-responsive design

## Phase 4 — White-label sales site (Months 7-12)

### Pages

- [P4] Sales collateral library (downloadable PDFs)

## Phase 5 — Authority site (Year 2+)

### Content & community
- [P5] Industry reports & whitepapers
- [P5] Annual State of Home Security report (data play, links to data moat)
- [P5] Dealer community / forum
- [P5] Affiliate program landing page
- [P5] Investor relations page (if raising capital)

### Internationalization
- [P5] Spanish-language site (Texas market opportunity)
- [P5] French-language site (if expanding to Quebec)

## Pages NOT to build

- Generic "Solutions" pages with no specific use case
- Vanity awards/recognition pages until we have real awards
- Team pages until we have a real team
- Long-form sales letter pages (low-trust pattern in security)

## Velocity expectations

| Phase | Pages live | Monthly visitors target |
|-------|-----------|------------------------|
| 1 | 11 | 500 |
| 2 | 25 | 5,000 |
| 3 | 60+ | 25,000 |
| 4 | 80+ | 75,000 |

SEO compounds. Be patient. Most growth happens 6-12 months after content is published.
