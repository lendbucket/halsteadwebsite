# Halstead Security — Brand Voice & Copy Guide

The brand voice for Halstead Security is the most important asset on this site. Get it wrong and we sound like every other security company. Get it right and we sound like a legitimate national brand from day one.

## Brand positioning

**Halstead Security** is built on the trust customers used to give to companies their parents used. Heritage trade brand for the modern home — installed by a local professional, monitored by smart software, backed by people who answer the phone.

We are not a tech startup. We are a security company that happens to use modern technology.

## Voice principles

**Direct, not clever.** Cove and SimpliSafe are direct. Vivint and ADT try to be clever. Be like Cove.

**Confident, not boastful.** "Your home is protected" — not "We're the #1 ranked security provider."

**Plain English.** A 60-year-old homeowner is our reader. No jargon. No tech-bro language. No "AI-powered intelligent perimeter monitoring." Just "your camera tells you when someone's at your door."

**Local, not corporate.** When possible, name the city. "Halstead Houston serves the greater Houston area." Specificity builds trust.

**Heritage cues.** Reference longevity, professional installation, family ownership. Even though the company is new, the *category* is old. We're inheriting the trust of the trade, not inventing a new one.

## Words we use

- Protect, watch, guard (verbs)
- Home, family, neighborhood (nouns)
- Trusted, professional, dependable (adjectives)
- Local technician, certified installer (people)
- Monthly service, included monitoring (commercial terms)

## Words we avoid

- Disrupt, revolutionary, game-changing (tech-bro)
- Cutting-edge, next-generation, AI-powered (jargon)
- Solution, ecosystem, platform (in customer-facing copy)
- Smart (overused)
- Seamless (vague)
- Empower (corporate)

## Headline formulas that work

**Outcome-led:** "See who's at your door. From anywhere."
**Problem-led:** "Tired of porch piracy? So are we."
**Comparison-led:** "Real cameras. Real installation. No 36-month contracts."
**Proof-led:** "[X] homes protected across Texas. Yours next."

## Headline formulas that don't work

- "The future of home security is here." (vague, AI-tone)
- "Smart security solutions for the modern home." (jargon stack)
- "Halstead is reinventing security." (we aren't, and don't need to claim that)

1. **Locally installed.** A real technician comes to your home.
2. **Monthly, not 36-month.** Cancel any time after your first year.
3. **Real people, real support.** When you call, a person picks up.

## Photography direction

- Real homes, not stock houses
- Real installers, not models
- Texas/Sunbelt aesthetic for v1 (we're a Texas company first)
- Diverse families, not just suburban tropes
- Daylight scenes — security doesn't need to feel scary

## Photography to avoid

- Hooded burglars at windows (fear-based, dated)
- Security guards in tactical gear (wrong vibe entirely)
- Generic stock "smart home" CGI imagery
- Moms looking worriedly at their phones at night

## Color palette (working)

- **Primary:** Halstead Navy `#0F1B2D` (deep, trustworthy)
- **Accent:** Halstead Gold `#C9A547` (heritage, warm)
- **Supporting:** Cream `#F4F1EA` (warm off-white)
- **Detail:** Charcoal `#2D2D2D` (body text)
- **Success:** Forest `#2F5233`
- **Error:** Burgundy `#7A1F1F` (not bright red)

The palette signals heritage trade brand. Avoid the SaaS purple/teal palette entirely.

## Typography

- **Headings:** Serif (suggested: Source Serif Pro, Playfair Display, or similar) — heritage, dignified
- **Body:** Sans-serif (suggested: Inter, Source Sans Pro) — modern, readable
- **Combination signals:** Old-school authority + modern approachability

## Logo direction

- Wordmark with optional shield mark
- Serif typography for "Halstead"
- Optional small monogram "H" in shield form for app icons and favicon
- Two-color simplicity (Navy + Gold or Navy + Cream)
- Avoid: lightning bolts, eye icons, cameras, locks (all overdone)

## Forms

- Always say what happens next ("A local Halstead specialist will call within 1 business day.")
- Ask for the minimum needed
- Show progress for multi-step forms
- Honor "I'm just looking" with no-pressure copy

## Trust signals to add as they become real

- # of homes protected
- Years in business (start with founding date)
- States served
- BBB rating (after we have one)
- Customer review aggregate (after we have reviews)
- Industry certifications (after we have them)

## Don't fake it

If we don't have customer reviews yet, don't fake them. Use authentic placeholders or omit. Trust is harder to rebuild than to delay claiming.
